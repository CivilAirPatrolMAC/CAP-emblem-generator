Emblem Graphic Guidance

Use this tool to create a CAP emblem-style graphic with approved wing, region, or other authorized secondary artwork.

Guidelines, Standards, and Requirements

  • Use only approved secondary graphics and emblems.
  • Top text should normally read CIVIL AIR PATROL.
  • Keep bottom text concise for readability on the lower arc.
  • Review spacing, alignment, and contrast before downloading.
  • Do not upload unofficial, altered, or low-quality graphics.
